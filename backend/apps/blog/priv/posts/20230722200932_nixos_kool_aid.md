I'm surprised that I've been using [Nix and NixOS](https://nixos.org) full time since Jun 30, 2020.

I'm still not an expert by any means, especially relative to the rest of the extremely talented community... but I've become all-in on Nix and use it on literally everything I own.

It started with hearing about it and deciding to try it out during my usual bi-annual Linux distro-hopping sessions. I was initially drawn to it for how esoteric it felt in comparison to distributions I had previously used, but after getting my first setup feeling comfy and homey I knew it was the future.

Insofar as Linux goes, my distro-hopping condition has been cured! I'm also a very regular user of Windows as well as an occasional user of MacOS, and the best part is that I can share my configuration across all three big operating systems!

Since I'm such a shill for niche(\*) technology I like, I thought I'd share a high level overview of Nix, NixOS, my configuration, and the superpowers it gives me.
